White Tie

Welcome

Oxford's only white tie winter ball

On 30th November 2019, you are cordially invited to the triennial Merton College Winter Ball. Attire yourself in the finest white tie and step through our gates into a magical starlit wonderland. Sway through music-filled quads brimming with delicious food and unlimited drinks, dance in marquees glittering with stars, escape into one of our countless attractions and bask in the splendour of Oxford’s oldest college. At Merton we’ve been partying since 1264 and we’re thrilled to welcome you into the fold.

Music

Non-stop live music all night

From Oxford’s best bands to a major international headliner, the whole night will be marked by a soundtrack of the greatest live music. Sway to the classiest jazz, dance away in the silent disco, or simply sit back and relax to the soundtrack of the best Oxford has to offer. With our varied music selection across three stages, there’s sure to be something for everyone’s taste. Our main stage will feature local bands and DJs throughout the night, ending in an exciting international headliner. If that’s not quite your scene, Mob Quad will be turned into an intimate alternative stage, featuring the best of Oxford’s underground, techno and hip-hop talent throughout the night.

Entertainment

Spectacle, dazzle, wonder

We have pulled out all the stops to keep you entertained throughout the night. As Merton transforms into a starry winter wonderland, we invite you to escape into a world of extravagant entertainment. Relive your best childhood memories with dodgems, 90s arcade games, and funfair rides. Challenge your friends to a karaoke battle or try your hand at gambling in our very own casino. If you tire from the rides and games, recover in the toasty shisha tent, relax in front of a magic show, or watch a ballet performance.

Charity

Giving back

The Merton Winter Ball is proud to be participating in the What’s A Pound initiative, in which £1 from each ticket sold is donated to charity, and we are excited to see how much difference a small donation can make.

The receiving charities, selected by the Merton College JCR and MCR, are Students Supporting Street Kids and Sobell House.
